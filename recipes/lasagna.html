Lasagna Recipe

Description

Cook the beef in a pan until browned, then stir in the pasta sauce. In a baking dish, layer noodles, meat sauce, ricotta, and mozzarella. Repeat layers and finish with mozzarella on top. Bake at 180°C (350°F) for 30–40 minutes until bubbly and golden.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Marinara / Pasta sauce
  • Ricotta cheese(or cottage cheese)
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
